About

This is our story

We visited Palermo a couple of years ago and were surprised to find that there were no sushi restaurants around. We decided this needed to be changed and a few years later we came up with the idea of opening up our very own sushi restaurant in Palermo. Our main goal is to cater to tourists, families and corporate events. Our fish is caught in the mediterranean on a daily basis and served up to you.
